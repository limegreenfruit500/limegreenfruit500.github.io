Welcome to Jolly Baboon Beer and Computer Lounge, Charlotte’s most playful spot for craft beer, tech fun, and good times! Our baboon-themed lounge offers a unique atmosphere where you can enjoy locally brewed beers, creative cocktails, baboon-inspired snacks, and computer stations for gaming or productivity.

Whether you’re here to unwind after work, meet friends, or celebrate with a quirky tech twist, our lounge brings together the fun spirit of our baboon mascot and the best of Charlotte’s beverage and computer scene. Join us for laughter, great drinks, and a truly unforgettable experience!
