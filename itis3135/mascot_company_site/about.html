About Us

Jolly Baboon Beer and Computer Lounge is Charlotte’s one-of-a-kind destination where craft beer meets creativity and technology. Our playful baboon mascot inspires a welcoming space for everyone to relax, connect, and enjoy the best local brews while using top-notch computer stations for gaming, work, or learning.

Whether you’re a tech enthusiast, a beer lover, or just looking for a fun place to hang out, our lounge is designed to spark joy and curiosity. We blend the vibrant energy of our baboon theme with a commitment to quality, comfort, and community.

Qualifiers & Guidelines

  • To Drink: Must be 21+ with valid ID.
  • To Use Computers: All ages welcome; under 16 must be accompanied by an adult.
  • Reservations: Recommended for groups of 6+ or special events. Walk-ins always welcome!

Hours & Reservations

  • Monday - Thursday: 2:00 PM – 10:00 PM
  • Friday - Saturday: 12:00 PM – Midnight
  • Sunday: 12:00 PM – 8:00 PM
  • Reservations: Call us or book online for parties, gaming tournaments, or private events.
